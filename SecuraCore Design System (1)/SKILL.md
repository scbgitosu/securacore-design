## Quick reference

- **Name:** SecuraCore — Bend, Oregon
- **Tagline:** Technology Made Simple
- **Colors:** Orange `#E36F1E`, ink `#1D252D`, white, grey ramp (`#F2F2F2`, `#B0B0B0`, `#6B6B6B`). Orange is always load-bearing.
- **Type:** Posterama (display, all-caps, tracked) + Open Sans (body). Never substitute with Inter/Roboto.
- **Icons:** Use the supplied 13-icon product set from `assets/`. For UI chrome, Lucide at 1.5px stroke. No emoji.
- **Voice:** Clear, confident, local, helpful, professional. Plain language. "We" / "your home". No jargon.
- **Imagery:** Warm Bend/Central Oregon homes — stone, timber, big glass, dusk light. No cold corporate stock.

Everything else is in `README.md`.
