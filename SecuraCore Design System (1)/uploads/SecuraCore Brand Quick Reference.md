## Brand Voice

| Tone | Description |
|---|---|
| **Confident** | We know our craft — speak with authority |
| **Clear** | No jargon, no fluff — technology made simple |
| **Local** | Bend-rooted, Central Oregon proud |
| **Helpful** | Educate first, sell second |
| **Professional** | Premium service, not a commodity |

### Voice Do's
- Use plain language (assume the client knows nothing about tech)
- Lead with the benefit, not the feature
- Reference local places, lifestyle, and community
- Use "we" and "our team" — not "the company"

### Voice Don'ts
- Don't use heavy technical jargon in client-facing content
- Don't be salesy or pushy
- Don't use generic stock phrases ("industry-leading," "cutting-edge")
- Don't ignore the local angle

## Primary Customer Personas

### "The Bend Homeowner"
- Age 35–55
- Homeowner, often has a second property (vacation/rental)
- Values quality, reliability, and local expertise
- Outdoor lifestyle — Bend, Sunriver, Sisters area
- Willing to pay for premium if the trust is there
- Wants technology that works without babysitting

### "The Bend Business Owner"
- Restaurant, retail, office, or commercial property
- Wants security, AV, and Wi-Fi that doesn't fail
- Cares about reliability over price
- Responds to referrals and local reputation
